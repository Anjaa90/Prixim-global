Match-Trader Platform for Brokers by Prixim Global

Prixim Global offers Match-Trader, a comprehensive platform for brokerage businesses. It features advanced trading tools, integrated CRM, Client Office/Trader Room, and supports social and prop trading. Designed for all traders, it provides brokers with a complete solution.

Features of MatchTrader Brokerage With Prixim Global

Seamless Access

Match-Trader platform, powered by Progressive Web App (PWA) technology, ensures uninterrupted access across web, mobile, and desktop devices for smooth trading.

Matching Engine Technology

Match-Trader platform allows technology providers and exchange operators to connect seamlessly to matching engine technology via FIX API and a proprietary FIX Bridge.

White Labeling Solutions

Prixim Global’s Match-Trader platform provides extensive white labeling options, allowing brokers to customize their branded app with their own logo and name, helping them build a strong, unique brand.



What Would You Get With Match-Trader Brokerage

CRM System

Streamline the KYC process for clients with a smooth account opening experience. Receive real-time notifications on traders’ activities.


Back Office

Provides a solid foundation that integrates seamlessly with other modules in the BrokerTools suite, enhancing brokerage management.


Trader's Room

Redesigned to improve trader engagement, allowing easy management of files, trading accounts, portfolios, and transactions in a

Partner Management

Advanced tools for managing various partner types, from affiliates to Introducing Brokers (IBs), with comprehensive tracking for online and offline activities.

Asset Management (PAMM)

Risk Management
